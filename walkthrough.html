Meal Prep Made Easy

Yndo Bowl offers an innovative all-in-one solution that simplifies meal preparation, cooking, and storage.


Ceramic insulated outer layer and an integrated steel plate for induction cooking and microwaving, allows users to cook and heat meals directly in the bowl.

Insulation keeps the bottom cool to the touch for easy handling and maintains food temperature for long periods of time.
