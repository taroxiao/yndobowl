Problem and Solution Statement

Problem Statement

In today's fast-paced world, busy individuals struggle to find time for home-cooked meals, often resorting to unhealthy fast food options. Traditional cookware requires multiple utensils for meal prep, cooking, and storage, adding to the inconvenience and time consumption.

Solution Statement

Yndo Bowl offers an innovative all-in-one solution that simplifies meal preparation, cooking, and storage. Its unique design features a ceramic insulated outer layer and an integrated steel plate for induction cooking as well as microwaving, allowing users to cook and heat meals directly in the bowl. The bowl's insulation keeps the bottom cool to the touch for easy handling and maintains food temperature during transport, providing a convenient and time-saving solution for home cooking.
