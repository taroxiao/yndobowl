Revenue Model

Yndo Bowl operates on a licensing model where the product is sold to large retailers with large manufacturing facilities (ie Walmart).

This approach allows for:

  • Broad distribution and availability in major retail stores, both online and offline.
  • Access to a wider customer base without the need for extensive direct-to-consumer marketing efforts.
  • Reduced overhead costs associated with manufacturing and distribution, as these responsibilities are handled by the licensed retailers.
  • Potential for revenue generation through licensing fees and royalties based on sales performance.

By partnering with large retailers, Yndo Bowl can leverage their established distribution networks and customer reach, ensuring the product is accessible to the target audience while maintaining a sustainable revenue model.
